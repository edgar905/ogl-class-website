About One Giant Leap AI

We're your guide through the AI space.

One Giant Leap AI exists to make the AI transition tangible for working professionals. Not theory — working tools you can deploy in your role this week. We've trained hundreds of professionals across industries and built the curriculum around what actually sticks.

This isn't a general AI overview. Every week builds on the last: you go from understanding how models think, to co-working with Claude, to building and shipping real automation. You leave with a project, not just a certificate.

Classes run monthly, every first Wednesday, so you can join when the timing is right for you.
